Me Life-Style

Four Years Ago, I contracted mononucleosis and walking pneumonia. Though it wasn't life threatening, I was diagnosed with POTS, an autonomic heart condition that slows down my heart rate. My symptoms include chronic pain, exhaustion, headaches, chronic fatigue and IBS. It has taken me some time, but four years later, I am the best I have ever been. I play soccer, train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, bike, lift, and do yoga. On top of this, I'm gluten free, dairy free, red-meat free, and I see an accupuncturist.
